Beef bone broth

(https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6GHwG1y-NE0)

Ingredients:
- 5 pounds of grass-fed beef bones
  - knuckle (joint) bones have more gelatin
  - meaty and marrow bones for flavor
- 6 chicken feet
- 5 quarts of water (1 quart per pound of bone)
- Olive oil
- 1 yellow onion, cut into quarters and then halved
- 3 stalk of celery, chopped into 2 inch pieces
- 2 large carrots, about 1.5 cups in large chunks
- 1 head of garlic
- 2 tablespoon of peppercorns
- 3 dried bay leaves
- 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon salt

Instructions:
- Drizzle oil on bones on a baking sheet.
- Roast bones in oven at 400 degrees for 45 min to 1 hour.
- Roast vegetables in oven at 400 degrees for 30 min.
- Trim claws off chicken feet.
- Add bones, chicken feet and vegetables to pressure cooker.
- Cover bones with water.
- Cook for 4 hours in pressure cooker.
- Skim off fat, save separately as tallow.
- Fish out solids for easier straining.
- Strain liquid through a cheesecloth or coffee filter over a fine mesh strainer.
