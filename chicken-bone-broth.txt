Chicken bone broth

Ingredients:
- 2 chicken backs
- 3 shallots
- 1 head of garlic
- 1 thumb sized piece of gender
- Any other bones

Instructions:
- Roughly chop vegetables and chicken backs.
- Roast vegetables in oven at 400 degrees for 30 min.
- Cook vegetables and chicken backs in pressure cooker for 4 hours.
- Empty liquid from pressure cooker.  This is the first extraction.
- Cover bones with water and cook for 4 hours.  This is the second extraction.
